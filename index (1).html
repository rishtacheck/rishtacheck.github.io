Frequently asked

What families often ask us.

Q. Is this legal in India?
Yes. Every verification uses public records, institutional channels, and consented methods that comply with Indian law including the DPDP Act 2023.
Q. Will the subject find out?
No. All verification is discreet by design. Methods used don't alert the person being verified. Your inquiry remains confidential at every stage.
Q. What if nothing concerning is found?
That is the most common outcome — and the most valuable one. A clean report gives you confidence to proceed without doubt. Peace of mind is the product.
Q. How accurate is the verification?
We source from primary records and institutions. Every finding is documented. Where data cannot be verified definitively, we say so clearly rather than speculate.
Q. Do you handle NRI cases?
Yes. Our NRI Premium package handles cross-border verification — overseas employment, visa status, foreign assets, and international litigation via vetted partners.
Q. Can you access bank statements or call records?
No. We never access data requiring illegal means. Any firm promising bank statements or call records without consent is operating outside the law.
Q. How quickly can you start?
Same week as the consultation. With signed engagement letter and intake form, verification begins within 48 hours. Urgent delivery available at a surcharge.
Q. Who sees the final report?
Only you and named family members you authorize. Reports are encrypted, watermarked, and never shared with any third party — including the subject's family.
Q. What is your refund policy?
If we cannot deliver the verification scope agreed upon, we refund the unused portion. Detailed terms are in the engagement letter you sign at kickoff.
Q. What if we want more verification later?
Existing clients receive 30% off any follow-up verification within 12 months — including post-engagement monitoring or re-verification before the wedding.
